FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find clarity on consultations, fees, timelines, and how we work with clients across Kenya and beyond.

Do you offer initial consultations?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute discovery consultation for most matters. Complex commercial cases may require a more detailed paid briefing, which we will discuss upfront.

How are your fees structured?

We offer hourly, fixed-fee, and retainer arrangements depending on the matter. Fee structures and anticipated costs are agreed upon before engagement begins.

Can you represent clients outside Nairobi?

Absolutely. We represent clients across Kenya and handle cross-border matters. Virtual consultations and travel arrangements are available as needed.

What should I prepare for my meeting?

Bring any relevant documents, contracts, court papers, or timelines. If you're unsure, our intake team will guide you before the consultation.

Do you handle urgent matters?

Yes. For urgent matters, contact us via phone and mark your submission as emergency. Our rapid response team is on standby 24/7.
